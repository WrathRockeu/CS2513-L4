General:
  Digits:
    Click the digit buttons 
    to input a number. When 
    satisfied, click the 'P' 
    button to push the input
    onto the stack (which is 
    displayed on the right). 
    Repeat to input other 
    numbers. 
  Operands:
    Clicking on one of the operand 
    buttons will result in applying 
    that operand to the first one or 
    two numbers on the stack.
